Henry Lee is a Creative Director and Software Engineer Professional with an extensive background in visual arts, a BFA in Graphic Design - Web Design, 10yrs+ of experience in the Web Development field, Brand Strategy, E-commerce, Contemporary Business Systems, and Creative Entrepreneurship.

“

My purpose is to inspire creative solutions, & help empower people to realize the greatest version of who they are meant to be.

”

In 2017 Henry Lee founded The Artwear Gallery, a sacred creative space for creatives of all kinds to collaborate, gain exposure, and earn an income. He led a team of 13+ creative professionals in efforts to build the brand, market the art and fashion products, build out the e-commerce platform, and host “creative collab” events such as The Artwear Fashion Exhibition. Today he aims to utilize that same energy to develop greater platforms and solutions as a creative software engineer.
